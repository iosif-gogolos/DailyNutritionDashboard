Get set up

This app reads a CSV of your day — hydration, meals, activity, and sleep. You don't fill it in by hand: an AI writes it for you.

Here's the loop:

  1. Tell us your basics once (height, weight, goals, diet)
  2. We build a ready-to-paste prompt for ChatGPT / Claude
  3. The AI hands back a CSV → you drop it into the dashboard
  4. Repeat step 2–3 each morning to plan your day

Copy this, paste it into ChatGPT or Claude, and paste back whatever CSV it gives you.

Open ChatGPT →

Almost done:

  1. Copy the AI's reply — it should be plain CSV text, nothing else
  2. Save it as a .csv file, or paste it directly if your AI tool lets you download one
  3. Drop that file onto the upload zone on the dashboard

Tomorrow, open the hamburger menu → Daily CSV Prompt to jump straight back to step 3 with your saved profile.
